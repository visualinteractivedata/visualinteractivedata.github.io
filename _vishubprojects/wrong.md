Hi Edinburgh DataVis Community,

It’s our great pleasure to announce the open keynote for the international conference on data visualization and information design: Information+, on Nov 22nd, 2023 in Edinburgh. (https://informationplusconference.com)

This event is free and open to everyone but registration (through Eventbrite) is required. It will be given by designer, artist, and author Stefanie Posavec, one of the authors of the Dear Data project. Please, spread the word and reserve your place. This event will be in-person only.

If you have already registered for the full Info+ conference, you do not need to register again for the keynote as your place is safe!

Please register your place for the open keynote on Eventbrite
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/youre-doing-it-wrong-a-celebration-of-worst-practices-in-data-vis-tickets-745022701957?aff=oddtdtcreator

Also, check out the full conference program of talks, workshops, two more keynotes, as well as an exhibition and dinner. Regular conference registration is still open: https://informationplusconference.com

We are looking forward to welcome you at Info+!
